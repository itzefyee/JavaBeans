About Us

Coffee is the best.


We make coffee using Premium Arabica Coffee Bean.

Our company named JavaBeans has been founded since 2022 by our CEO Mr Efye Ching and three partners which are Mr Yong Zhen, Mr Ja Man and Mr Shi Tao. We established this company because we found it difficult to get a coffee or drink when we are busy and this problem also appears in public. Hence, we want to provide quality coffee and drink to the public. Our coffee bean supplier is providing the premium coffee bean to us which is Arabica Coffee Bean. Furthermore, the raw materials were also sourced from worldwide. So our company's product can take the public environment to the next level which is relaxing and productive.
